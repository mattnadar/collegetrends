Examining Trends on r/college to try and learn about student needs
By digging into what students are talking about here, we can learn a lot about what’s really on their minds. Whether it’s student loans, mental health, finding a job, or just surviving finals, these posts give us a raw, unfiltered look at the challenges and questions students are facing.
Methods
To accomplish this, I used PRAW, a Python API that lets you interact with reddit data.

My initial idea was to scrape a bunch of posts from r/college, and then do an analysis on common words between many posts.
